Draw your heart out

Draw your heart out is a drawing app, where you can draw with your mouse. You can choose between 3 different colors and 3 different sizes of the brush. You can also erase your drawing.

Draw your heart out is made with HTML, CSS and JavaScript.

Gif Emotions

Gif Emotions is a website, where you can search for gifs by searching, but also with filters such as offset, limit, rating, and language

Gif Emotions is made with HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and API.

IMDB clone

IMDB clone is a website, where you can search for movies. You can search for movies by title, year, rating, votes and duration. You can also get newer movies.

IMDB clone is made with HTML, CSS and JavaScript.

Find Luffy

Find Luffy is a game, where you have to find Luffy. You can move around with the arrow keys. If you find Luffy, you win. If you find a bomb, you lose.

Find Luffy is made with HTML, CSS and JavaScript.

Imprecise calculator

Imprecise calculator is a calculator, where you can add, subtract, multiply and divide.

Imprecise calculator is made with HTML, CSS and JavaScript.

Tic Tac Toe

Tic Tac Toe is a game, where you can play against the computer. You can choose between 3 different levels of difficulty.

Tic Tac Toe is made with HTML, CSS and JavaScript.
